The history of the Korean Peninsula goes far back to well...a long time ago
But for the sake your your sanity
We shall briefly cover the Joseon Dynasty(1392-1897)

After entering the Joseon Dynasty, the people settled into a predetermined caste system consisting of the:
양반, 중인, 상민, and 천민 (ranked highest to lowest)

All was fine until Japanese and Manchu forces invaded Korea in the 16th and 17th century
Korea soon isolated itself from the world until the US decided to initiate international trade
However Joseon became a battlefield for the coutries adjacent,
and Japanese forces ultimately annexed Joseon and the Korean Peninsula entered dark ages.

But that's enough of the depressing stories
Let's explore the great achievements of Joseon that allowed Korea to prosper into a supernation today

First we have King Sejong (May 7, 1397–April 8, 1450).

As a brilliant young scholar who took a great interest in in studies, King Sejong fouded many great inventions that would revolutionize Korea

An accurate sun dial

A precise rain gauge

한글, the Korean Language

Finally we have admiral 이순신 (1545-1598)

The brave leader fought 23 battles against the Japanese invaders of Korea between 1592 and 1598
He won every one of them without losing a single ship.
In 14 of these battle not a single Japanese ship survived.
